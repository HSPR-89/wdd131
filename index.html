Costa Rica

Costa Rica is a small yet incredibly diverse country in Central America, known for its stunning natural beauty, rich biodiversity, and commitment to environmental sustainability. It is bordered by Nicaragua to the north, Panama to the south, the Pacific Ocean to the west, and the Caribbean Sea to the east. With a landscape that ranges from lush rainforests and volcanic mountains to pristine beaches, Costa Rica offers a unique blend of ecosystems that make it a haven for nature lovers and wildlife enthusiasts. The country is renowned for its "pura vida" lifestyle, which reflects a relaxed, positive attitude toward life, and its stable democratic government, which has been in place for over a century. Costa Rica has no standing army, prioritizing instead investment in education and healthcare, and is often recognized as one of the happiest countries in the world. Its economy is largely driven by tourism, agriculture, and technology, and the country is also a leader in renewable energy, generating a significant portion of its power from hydroelectric, wind, and solar sources. Costa Ricans are known for their friendly, welcoming nature, and the country’s blend of modernity with a deep respect for tradition and nature makes it a truly unique and enriching place to live and visit.
